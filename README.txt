This is a flashcard program for word learning. Enter the language you want to learn and a language you know, first the card will show you the foreign word for
3 seconds, then it will show the word in your known language. If you know the word click the "check" button and that word will be removed from the list of words.
Click the X button and it will stay in the list.

To reset the program, go to DATA folder and delete "words_to_learn.csv" and "usr_len.txt" files.
